Features

SignSage integrates ASL to text conversion with innovative features to bridge the communication gap between the Deaf and hearing communities. It promotes inclusivity in digital interactions and empowers individuals with diverse communication needs. Discover our cutting-edge functionalities for effective communication.


About Us

About the project


SignSage is a pioneering initiative dedicated to fostering inclusivity and empowerment. Rooted in the mission to bridge communication divides between disabled minorities and the digital landscape, SignSage leverages cutting-edge technology to dismantle barriers. Our unwavering objective is to ensure equitable opportunities for all to seamlessly connect, communicate, and flourish within social and virtual spheres. With SignSage, we are championing a future defined by accessibility and inclusiveness.

Who We Are


"We" are a professional team driving SignSage forward. As innovators, advocates, and allies, we're committed to empowering disabled minorities in the digital sphere.


What We Do


Our mission revolves around the development and implementation of SignSage, a comprehensive solution designed to address the communication challenges faced by disabled minorities within virtual environments.
